CONFERENCE THEMES

Here is our Conference's Theme

Ultrasound induces various chemical and physical effects in liquids by acoustic cavitation phenomenon. The ultrasonic technology is ecofriendly, and economically viable to operate in numerous industries. The applications of ultrasound includes wastewater treatment, nanomaterial synthesis, polymerization, emulsification, crystallization, degassing, food processing & preservation, imaging, cutting & drilling, medical, etc. The 5th AOSS promises to lay new avenues by reviewing recent developments in the field of sonochemistry. Chemical and physical operating parameters influencing cavitation threshold, activity, intensity and associated effects (experimental and theoretical); chemical and physical dosimetry of cavitational activity; frequency effect on sonochemistry; single bubble sonochemistry; sonochemistry in non-cavitating conditions; reactor and probe design; scale-up. The topics are unlimited other than the above.

  • Cavitation, Sonochemistry & Sonoluminescence
  • Sonoprocessing (e.g., Dairy, Food, etc.)
  • Materials Science (e.g., Colloids and Nanomaterials, etc.)
  • Environmental Sonochemistry
  • Ultrasound in Medical Applications
  • Other Related Areas
Other themes: A separate symposium will be conducted all the latest research areas in Chemical Engineering including Energy, Water, Environment, Transport processes, Food & Pharmaceutical Engg., Circular Economy, Instrumentation and Process Control, Biotechnology, Nnaotechnology, CFD, Advanced Materials
